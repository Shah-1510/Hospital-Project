Welcome to Dr.Doo Little Clinic International Hospital Dr.Doo Little Clinic is a hospital with a concept built on providing the best of health to every citizen. Started in 2009, it is an effort to take the healthcare to the next level. The comfortable atmosphere that it offers causes a person to feel the peace. Its vibes and decor rival the best anywhere and its infrastructure including diagnostics (laboratory & imaging) is second to none. Based on seven operation theaters that are equipped with the latest technology, its surgical field is able to cater for most types of surgical treatments. It also offers the latest in haemo dialysis on an outpatient basis. Its ER/Emergency gives an impression of a friendly and welcoming place for patients and relatives both. The staff in all out-patient and in-patient departments is friendly, competent and caring. The hospital has a special Patient Coordination Department that coordinates with doctors, nurses, and interpreters who serve the patients. It ensures the comfortable and smooth provision of services to each and every patient entering its doors. Located in the 16th street boston. The building radiates its uniqueness in design; its colors and ambiance spell the word ‘Healing’ so apt for a hospital. In-house comfort and relaxation is beyond star rating. A team of most competent consultants along with their crew are always ready to relieve your pain in no time. Much above the equipment, technology, design and structure of our facility, our main strength is our staff. We endeavor to handpick Simply the best.
